about me

outside of content creation, i enjoy playing video games, listening to music, and learning new hobbies.

my journey

editing

For as long as I can remember, I always thought of myself as a very independent person. Instead of being the audience, I wanted to be the one to create, and that's what kickstarted my creative journey.

I began posting to YouTube in 2016 as hobby, and I honestly had no idea what I was doing. My videos were recorded on a 10-year-old camcorder, and there was no editing involved.

Then, in 2022, after going through multiple failed channels and hiatuses, I set out on a lifelong mission to constantly improve my videos and commit to that task.

Despite the limited success of my solo videos, I struck a life-changing deal with well-known YouTuber and game developer Jaden Williams in 2024, which propelled me into this industry farther than I could have ever imagined. Before this, I struggled to find work due to a lack of pre-existing work to show for myself. Now, I love to do editing on Fiverr as a side hustle with tons of five-star reviews to show for it!

coding

There was a while where I was not focused on editing, and I honed in on my coding skills instead. This started on Roblox in 2018, where I filled my free time with small side projects to learn the fundamentals of Luau. My "breakthrough" arrived in 2022, where I released a parody game of "3008" that has amassed over 350K+ visits throughout its lifetime. I still maintain that game to this day, and even published a UI remaster in 2026.

I then picked up Visual Studio Code in 2023, and throughout that year I would go on to learn Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I went on to release a highly successful online gaming website and a personal blog hosted via Replit, but these are sadly not available anymore since my account is no longer active.

The following year, I enrolled myself in a Java programming course to stay in touch with my coding ability. However, I already found my career in editing, and decided that Java was a redundant language that I would not use later in life.

So, up until recent years (2025), I would work on other projects to improve my development even more. My only notable game of 2026 was created as part of a collaborative "game-jam" contest, where I took the role of the lead scripter, UI designer, and music composer in a team of three.

graphics

I always had a grasp of graphic design motifs, since I would use Pixlr to make the thumbnails for my older Roblox games and YouTube videos. While a free alternative was adequate at the time, I now use Adobe Illustrator for all graphic design work after learning the program through a course I took from 2024 to 2025, and continued in 2026.
