why do i recommended fiverr to solo creators?

for one-time or non-scheduled orders, fiverr is preferred because each project is automatically added to my portfolio (plus, every order gets me closer to "Top Rated" status).

large-scale creators ("brands") generally pay more because video editing is a very time-consuming task, depending on the content. my fiverr prices are extremely competitive since i get to set the rules, but in a professional environment, delivery times are often much shorter, with the client's style usually being strict and not up to interpretation.

if i'm going to be part of your brand, i expect to share some revenue from it as well.

do you take recurring orders?

yes! in fact, i offer two types of subscription deals on my page: 3 and 6 months, with 10% and 15% off all subsequent orders respectively.

can you design thumbnails for fiverr clients?

absolutely! thumbnails are priced at $10 on my page.
